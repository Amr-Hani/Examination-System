Rules before start exam

Prepare thoroughly

by checking technical requirements, gathering materials, and setting up a distraction-free environment.

Log in early

follow instructions carefully, and ensure all required tools (e.g., webcam, microphone) are enabled.

Stay focused

during the exam, manage your time wisely, and avoid prohibited actions to maintain academic integrity.

Submit properly

and confirm your exam has been successfully received before logging out.

Stay calm and honest

throughout the process to ensure a smooth and fair online exam experience.
